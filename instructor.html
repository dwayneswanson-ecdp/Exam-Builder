How the online exam will look

Overview

Exam settings

Exams are configured with a school year, class, subject, duration and time zone. In a future version, these settings will be synchronised with the school’s systems.

  • Desktop or web application.
  • Time interval or specific exam time.
  • Options such as incompressible duration, anonymous correction and copy/paste rules.
Question type

QCU & QCM (single or multiple choice)

MCQ questions can include text, images, videos or graphs. Instructors can set points, penalties and decide whether to show or hide the scoring scale.

  • Create questions one by one or copy existing ones.
  • Randomise choices and control penalties for wrong answers.
  • Define difficulty level and estimated time per question.
Question type

Open-ended responses

Open questions are ideal for essays, case studies and situational exercises. Students answer directly in the platform’s editor.

  • Free-text responses with rich formatting options.
  • Useful for evaluating reasoning, argumentation and writing.
Structure

Exam question builder

The exam interface allows instructors to mix different question types and structure the exam in parts. The beta page you are using simply produces the content; the platform will eventually mirror this structure.
