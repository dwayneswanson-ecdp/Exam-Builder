For schools & instructors

Secure, lightweight online exams – without central storage

This beta version standardises exam formats and helps instructors build online exams while keeping full control: exams are generated and sent directly by email, not stored on a server.

Create an exam

Why this platform?

  • Harmonise exam practices across classes and teachers.
  • No central storage: each exam is generated and emailed to the instructor.
  • Support for QCU/QCM and open-ended questions, with multimedia options.
  • Simple workflow so instructors can focus on pedagogy, not tools.

Benefits of the beta

For the school

  • Lightweight, privacy-friendly beta: exams are not stored centrally.
  • Standardised exam format across different modules and classes.
  • Clear exam templates that can be reused and adapted.
  • Easy to extend later towards a more integrated solution.

For instructors & students

  • Guided “Create exam” flow with minimal technical steps.
  • Multiple question types: QCU, QCM, and open-ended responses.
  • Control over difficulty, timing and scoring rules.
  • Exams received by email so instructors can archive them however they prefer.
